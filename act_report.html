Act report¶

The analysis only focusses on a three main points:

Wether different dog stages, a higher number of images, or a longer text result in a higher score or higher favorite count.

Due to reasons mentioned in the analysis it was expected that favorite count will be a more reliable source of insights, and this proved to be the case.

Plot 2 showed that the dog stage “puppy” has a more than 75% higher number of favorite counts, only followed by dog stage “doggo” which has about 30% less favorite count but sill 50% more favorite counts than the two formerly mentioned.

Now while the master dataframe resulted in a bit less than 1000 rows, due to the desire to have favorite count added to each row for analysis, the rows however can be regarded as qualitative insightful as most possible measures where taken to have as cleaned rows as possible.

Overall it con be suggested that if desired a high score in WeRateDogs at least 2 images of a “puppo” dog should be posted with a text of about 150 characters, this will statistically give the highest chance of a positive feedback.
